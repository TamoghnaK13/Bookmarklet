Bookmarklet Archive

The most extensive bookmarklet archive list.

Bookmarklets are short snippets of JavaScript that can be executed by clicking on a, well, bookmark. That's where they get the name from. I have compiled a list of some free and public bookmarklets here for anyone to use. I don't remember the authors of some of them, but I claim no ownership over any of the following bookmarklets (Thanks to GBasil @ GBasil.dev for many of the listed ones). To install a bookmarklet, simply drag it to your bookmark bar, and then you can click it to activate it.

Changes the title and the favicon (the name of the tab and the icon) to whatever you set here. You can use a url of the website that you want to use the favicon of, eg youtube.com. You can also leave them empty if you like.

Refresh rate

How often to set the favicon and title, in milliseconds. This is for when a site reverts its title and favicon. "1000" would be every second, "60000" would be every minute. Defaults to 1000.
